# Responsible Social Media Use Checklist (For College Students)

## ✅ Do's
-  Think before you post – ensure your content is respectful and appropriate.
-  Maintain privacy settings to protect personal information.
-  Use social media for learning, networking, and skill-building.
- Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation.
-  Be respectful in comments and discussions.

##  Don'ts
- Do not share sensitive personal information (address, phone number, passwords).
- Avoid cyberbullying, trolling, or engaging in online conflicts.
- Don’t post inappropriate or offensive content.
- Avoid wasting excessive time scrolling mindlessly.
- Don’t believe or spread fake news without checking sources.
